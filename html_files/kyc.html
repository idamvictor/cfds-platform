FAQ
How long does verification take?
Most verifications complete within 1–3 business days. Automated checks run instantly. You may trade with Basic tier limits while pending.
Which documents are accepted?
Passport, National ID, or Driver's License for identity. Utility bill, bank statement, or government letter (within 3 months) for address proof.
Can I trade while verifying?
Yes, you can trade immediately with Basic tier limits ($2,000/day, $10,000/month). Full limits activate upon approval.
Why do you need my address?
Address verification is a FINMA regulatory requirement under AML/KYC guidelines and is standard across all regulated financial platforms.
What if my document is rejected?
You'll receive an email explaining the reason. You can resubmit immediately. Common reasons include blurry images, expired documents, or mismatched information.
