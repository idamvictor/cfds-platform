Withdrawal FAQ
What is the 10% early exit fee and when does it apply?
The 10% early exit fee applies to the gross amount for any exit within the 6-month hold period. Withdrawing $50,000 early means $5,000 is deducted — you receive $45,000 before taxes.
After 6 months, withdrawals are completely fee-free.
Can I withdraw a portion of my staked portfolio?
Partial withdrawals are allowed. The 10% fee applies only to the withdrawn portion. Your remaining balance stays invested and actively managed. Minimum partial withdrawal is $1,000.
What happens to my open positions on early withdrawal?
All active portfolio positions are immediately force-closed at current market prices. Unrealised gains are added; unrealised losses are crystallised and deducted from your payout.
Are there tax implications on early withdrawal?
Early exits may trigger CGT in your home country. Retirement Staking™ is structured in a crypto-friendly jurisdiction to minimise exposure, but local laws vary. Consult a tax professional.
Long-term holders (5+ years) benefit from the most favourable tax-efficient exit structure.
How long does an early withdrawal take?
24–48 hours after approval, including force-close of positions. After the 6-month hold period, standard withdrawals process within 1–3 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is Retirement Staking™ and how does it differ from regular trading?
Retirement Staking™ is a long-term crypto investment vehicle designed for wealth accumulation. Unlike active trading, your portfolio is professionally managed with automatic rebalancing, compound growth, and tax-efficient structuring. There are no trading fees — the only cost is the 10% early exit fee if you withdraw within the first 6 months.
After the 6-month hold period, all withdrawals are completely fee-free.
How is my portfolio managed?
Your funds are allocated across a diversified basket of blue-chip digital assets (BTC, ETH, SOL, and others). Our fund managers use algorithmic and discretionary strategies to maximise returns while managing risk. The portfolio is rebalanced automatically based on market conditions.
Where are my funds stored?
All funds are held in FINMA-licensed Swiss cold storage vaults with institutional-grade security. Assets are insured up to $250,000 per account. Multi-signature authorization is required for any fund movement.
What is the 6-month hold period?
The 6-month hold period begins from your first deposit. During this time, early withdrawals incur a 10% early exit fee. After 6 months, you can withdraw any amount at any time with zero fees. This hold period exists to ensure portfolio stability and optimal returns for all participants.
What returns can I expect?
Historical performance shows an average of 15–25% annual growth, though past performance doesn't guarantee future results. The tax-efficient structure means more of your gains compound over time compared to traditional taxable accounts. Our 10-year projections are based on 20% average annual growth.
Long-term holders (5+ years) historically see the strongest risk-adjusted returns.
