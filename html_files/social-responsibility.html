Sustainability

Our Path to
Net Zero by 2028

We are committed to eliminating our carbon footprint across all operations, data centers, and supply chains.

100% Renewable Energy
Swiss Hydroelectric Powered
Renewable Energy Transition
All of our server infrastructure has migrated to Swiss hydroelectric power, with solar agreements covering our Zurich headquarters and Geneva offices.
Carbon Offset Purchasing
We purchase verified carbon offsets through Gold Standard certified projects in Brazil and Indonesia to neutralize emissions we cannot yet eliminate.
Hardware Recycling Program
Old servers and hardware are responsibly recycled or donated to schools in developing nations rather than being sent to landfill.
